A lightweight and highly customizable multi-purpose theme that makes it easy for anyone to create their perfect website.

== Description ==
BlogHash is the perfect pick for bloggers seeking a lightweight, customizable theme that suits them just right. With plenty of options to adjust colors and typography, making your site unique is a breeze. It's SEO friendly and fully compatible with WPML, Gutenberg, Elementor, WooCommerce, and supports translation and RTL. Live preview: https://peregrine-themes.com/bloghash/#demos.

1. Log into your WordPress Dashboard and go to Appearance Â» Themes and click the "Add New" button.
2. Type in "Bloghash" in the search field and press the "Enter" key on your keyboard.
3. Click the ""Install" and then "Activate" button to activate BlogHash theme on your site.
